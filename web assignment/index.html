History For Tomorrow

History for Tomorrow is a web-based platform created to educate the current generation about the ongoing crisis of Gender-Based Violence (GBV) in South Africa. It serves as both an educational resource and a memorial to honour the lives lost.The website raises awareness by sharing real experiences and aims to equip individuals with the knowledge and responsibility to challenge GBV. It also provides support through access to resources and helplines for those affected. Additionally, the platform promotes a safe, inclusive environment while encouraging respect, equality, and collective responsibility in addressing GBV.
